    Compliance

    Company secretaries help ensure that the company adheres to relevant laws, regulations, and corporate governance standards. They monitor changes in legislation, prepare and file necessary documents, and maintain statutory registers.

    Board Support

    Company secretaries assist the board of directors by organizing and preparing board meetings, drafting agendas and minutes, and providing guidance on corporate governance matters. They facilitate effective communication between the board and management.

    Shareholder Relations

    Company secretaries manage interactions with shareholders, ensuring compliance with disclosure requirements, organizing annual general meetings, and handling shareholder communications. They also assist with share issuances, transfers, and dividend payments.

    Company Formation and Administration

    Company secretaries can assist with the formation of new companies, ensuring compliance with legal requirements and registering the company with relevant authorities. They also manage the maintenance of company records, including shareholder and director information.

    Legal and Regulatory Compliance

    Company secretaries keep track of changes in company law and regulations, advising the board and management on compliance matters. They ensure that company policies and procedures align with legal and regulatory requirements.

    Corporate Governance

    Company secretaries play a vital role in promoting good corporate governance practices. They assist in the development of corporate governance frameworks, help establish board committees, and monitor compliance with codes of conduct and ethical standards.

    Risk Management and Reporting

    Company secretaries assist in identifying and managing corporate risks. They ensure accurate and timely reporting to regulatory authorities, shareholders, and other stakeholders.
